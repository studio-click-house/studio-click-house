## Platform

web

## Users

Creative agencies, e-commerce brands, photographers, production teams, and brands looking for premium, high-volume image editing, high-end beauty/fashion retouching, commercial video editing, and photorealistic 3D/CGI rendering.

## Product Purpose

Studio Click House provides high-end image post-production, video editing, and 3D modeling services. Success means delivering flawless, production-ready assets that elevate the client's visual branding, maintaining rapid turnarounds, and providing an elegant, premium interactive experience on the marketing website.

## Positioning

High-end post-production craftsmanship combined with technological efficiency. Unlike generic outsourcing agencies, Studio Click House focuses on bespoke, editorial-level quality, precise hand-drawn clipping paths, complex jewelry retouching, and photorealistic CGI staging.

## Operating Context

Marketing web presence where prospective clients evaluate quality, view work samples, check pricing structures, and contact the studio to initiate high-volume or bespoke production contracts.

## Capabilities and Constraints

- **Capabilities**: High-end editorial/e-commerce retouching, background removal, ghost mannequin, jewelry retouching, video post-production (grading, cutdowns), and 3D product modeling/rendering.
- **Constraints**: Private VPS deployment using `@sveltejs/adapter-node`, Cloudflare R2 for media storage, Svelte 5 runes (where needed), Tailwind CSS v4, GSAP with ScrollTrigger and Lenis smooth scrolling, Three.js for lightweight WebGL/3D effects. Performance-first, accessible, SEO-friendly, and mobile-optimized.
